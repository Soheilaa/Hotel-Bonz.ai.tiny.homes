Our Garden Rooms

These garden suites are spacious and can accommodate up to 2 adults and 2 children. Located on the ground floor these light and airy suites include a private garden which opens out onto the hotel gardens. These suites have a substantial king-size bed and a full en-suite bathroom with a bath and thermostatic overhead shower.
